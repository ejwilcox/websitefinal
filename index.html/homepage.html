Description

August 2024 I applied to be part of the JSP 1/30 Semester in Patagonia course through NOLS (National Ourdoor Leadership School). It was an eighty day course with 4 main sections: Sea Kayaking, Hiking, Rock Climbing and ISGE (Independent Student Group Expedition) Looking back, that choice became one of the most meaningful I’ve ever made. I now belong to a global network of people who are similary passionate about outdoor experience. As this NOLS course progressed, I began to journal a lot. During sea kayaking it was difficult since it was almost always raining, but once the hiking section started my entries became longer. I am grateful that I began to prioritize this. Now I have a physical reminder of some of the best days of my life. It is a habit that I've kept since returing home to Madison.
